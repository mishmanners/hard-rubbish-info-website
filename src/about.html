What is RePurposeful?

Welcome to RePurposeful, your ultimate guide to giving unwanted items a new life, or finding the correct disposal for your hard rubbish. This website was originally built for a hackathon. If you'd like to add information to the website, please fill out the "Contact Us" form.

What is Hard Rubbish?

Hard rubbish refers to large items that cannot be disposed of in regular garbage collections. This includes furniture, appliances, electronics, batteries, paints, building materials, and other bulky items.

Additional resources:

Proper disposal of hard rubbish is crucial for environmental sustainability. It helps reduce landfill waste and promotes recycling and reuse of materials. Here are some global resources for waste management:

  • EPA - Recycling and Waste
  • The Junkluggers - alternative uses
  • UNEP - Global Waste Management Outlook
  • Global Citizen - reuse household items
  • Earth911
