What is Hard Rubbish?

Hard rubbish refers to large items that cannot be disposed of in regular garbage collections. This includes furniture, appliances, and other bulky items.

Importance of Proper Disposal

Proper disposal of hard rubbish is crucial for environmental sustainability. It helps reduce landfill waste and promotes recycling and reuse of materials.

Guidelines for Disposal

  • Check local regulations for hard rubbish collection dates.
  • Ensure items are clean and free of hazardous materials.
  • Place items out for collection on the designated day.
